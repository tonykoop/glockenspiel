# Notes: Twinkle Twinkle Little Star

**Provenance:** Melody is the traditional French tune "Ah vous dirai-je, maman" (18th century, PD).
The "Twinkle" lyrics were written by Jane Taylor, published 1806, PD in the US.
Mozart's Variations K.265/300e (1781) popularized the tune — also PD.

**Range check (glockenspiel):** C5–A5. Well within any glockenspiel's range.

**Practice tips:**
1. Master the first 4 bars alone — that's the full "Twinkle" verse.
2. The middle section (bars 5–8) is the "bridge" — same notes, different order.
3. Bars 9–12 repeat bars 1–4. The whole song is just two ideas.

**Mallet technique:** Use alternating strokes (R-L-R-L). Don't play two consecutive
notes with the same hand unless the notes are far apart.
