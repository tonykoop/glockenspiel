# Notes: Ode to Joy

**Provenance:** 4th movement theme from Beethoven's Symphony No. 9 in D minor, Op. 125 (1824).
Beethoven died 1827; work entered public domain long ago. This is the unaccompanied melody only.

**Range check (glockenspiel):** C#5–G5. Fits comfortably on any glockenspiel.

**Practice tips:**
1. The melody repeats almost exactly — learn bars 1–4 and you know 75% of the piece.
2. The "bridge" section (bars 9–12) has the most movement — practice it slowly first.
3. The dotted quarter + eighth rhythm (e'3 d') feels natural if you count "1-and-2-AND-3".
