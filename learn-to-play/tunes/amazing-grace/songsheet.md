# Amazing Grace — Songsheet

**Key:** G major  **Tempo:** ♩= 80  **Meter:** 3/4  **Difficulty:** ★★☆  
**Range:** D5–D6  **Source:** John Newton, "Olney Hymns" 1779; tune trad. PD

## Music
```
X:4 T:Amazing Grace M:3/4 L:1/4 Q:1/4=80 K:G
g'2 | g'3/2 b'/ g' e' | e'2 d' | d'3/2 g'/ b'2 | g'3 |
b'3/2 d''/ b' g' | a'2 b' | g'3/2 e'/ g' d' | g'3 |
(verse 2 same as verse 1)
```

## Notes
- 3/4 waltz feel — count "1-2-3" steadily, placing the pickup note just before "1".
- The melody stays in the G5–D6 range — very comfortable on glockenspiel.
- The grace note pairs (3/2 + /) can be simplified to plain quarter notes for beginners.
