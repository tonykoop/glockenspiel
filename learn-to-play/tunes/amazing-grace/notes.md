# Notes: Amazing Grace

**Provenance:** Lyrics by John Newton, published in "Olney Hymns" (1779), PD.
Tune "New Britain" is a traditional American folk melody, origin unknown, PD.
This arrangement is freshly written from the lead-sheet melody — no modern arranger rights.

**Range check:** G5–D6. Upper note (D6) requires confident mallet control.

**Practice tips:**
1. The pickup note (g') starts just before bar 1. Think of it as the "upbeat."
2. The signature dotted rhythm (g'3/2 b'/) = dotted quarter + eighth. Practice: long-short, long-short.
3. At ♩= 80, the 3/4 feel is slow and stately — don't rush.
