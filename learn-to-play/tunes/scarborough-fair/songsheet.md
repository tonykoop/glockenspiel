# Scarborough Fair — Songsheet

**Key:** D dorian (D natural minor with raised 6th)  **Tempo:** ♩= 76
**Meter:** 3/4  **Difficulty:** ★★★  **Range:** C5–D6
**Source:** Traditional English ballad, public domain

## Music
```
X:5 T:Scarborough Fair M:3/4 L:1/4 Q:1/4=76 K:Dmin
a' | d'' a' f' | d'3/2 e'/ f' | e'2 c' | d'3 |
f' g' a' | c''2 a' | g'2 f' | a'3 |
(repeat for each verse)
```

## Modal note
Scarborough Fair is in **D dorian** — similar to D natural minor, but with B-natural
instead of B-flat. This gives it the distinctively "ancient" folk sound.
On glockenspiel, play all B notes natural (not flat).

## Notes
- Slower than the other tunes — the modal color needs space to breathe.
- The pickup note (a') begins just before bar 1, leading into the leap up to d''.
- Practice the leap d'–d'' (an octave) carefully — it's the signature gesture.
