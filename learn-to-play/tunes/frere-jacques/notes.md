# Notes: Frère Jacques

**Provenance:** Traditional French children's song / round, 17th century. Public domain worldwide.
Sometimes attributed to Guillaume-Gabriel Nivers or Jean-Philippe Rameau, but origin is uncertain.

**Range check:** G5–A5. Comfortably within glockenspiel range.

**Practice tips:**
1. The first phrase (C D E C) is the seed of the whole piece — all other bars derive from it.
2. For the eighth-note run, practice: G A G F E C slowly (♩= 60) until smooth.
3. As a round, the glockenspiel's sustain helps create the overlapping harmonic texture.
