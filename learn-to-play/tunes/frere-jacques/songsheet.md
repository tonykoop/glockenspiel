# Frère Jacques — Songsheet

**Key:** C major  **Tempo:** ♩= 108  **Meter:** 4/4  **Difficulty:** ★★☆
**Range:** G5–A5  **Source:** Traditional French (17th c.), public domain

## Music
```
X:3 T:Frère Jacques M:4/4 L:1/4 Q:1/4=108 K:C
|: c' d' e' c' | c' d' e' c' | e' f' g'2 | e' f' g'2 :|
|: g'/ a'/ g'/ f'/ e' c' | g'/ a'/ g'/ f'/ e' c' | c' G' c'2 | c' G' c'2 :|
```

## Round form
This is a 4-voice round. After player 1 completes bar 1, player 2 starts from the beginning.
On glockenspiel, play alone or in a round with other instruments.

## Notes
- Bars 1–2 repeat exactly — once you learn bar 1, bar 2 is free.
- The eighth-note run (g'/ a'/ g'/ f'/) requires quick mallet work. Practice slowly.
- The drop to G' (G5) at the end gives the tune its rounding resolution.
