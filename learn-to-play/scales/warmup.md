# Glockenspiel Warm-Up: C Major Scale Walk

Play each scale ascending then descending. Use alternating mallets (R=right, L=left).

```
X:1
T:C Major Scale Warm-Up
M:4/4
L:1/8
K:C
c'2 d'2 e'2 f'2 | g'2 a'2 b'2 c''2 | c''2 b'2 a'2 g'2 | f'2 e'2 d'2 c'2 |
```

## Pentatonic warm-up (5-note scale, easier for beginners)
Remove F and B — play only C D E G A:
```
X:2
T:C Pentatonic Warm-Up
M:4/4
L:1/8
K:C
c'2 d'2 e'2 g'2 | a'2 g'2 e'2 d'2 | c'2 e'2 g'2 a'2 | g'2 e'2 d'2 c'2 |
```

**Practice tip:** Play each note clearly and let it ring before moving on. Glockenspiel
sustain is short — connect notes by moving fast between them.
