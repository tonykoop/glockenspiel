# Image-Gen Prompts

Concept image only; not fabrication authority. Do not include cut-ready dimensions, bar lengths, node-hole positions, frame CNC geometry, tuning tables, or toolpaths.

## Hero Prompt

Photorealistic 3/4 product photograph of a finished 25-bar chromatic glockenspiel: bright 6061-style aluminum flat bars arranged C5-C7 in a piano-keyboard layout, naturals in the front row and raised sharps behind, each bar suspended by cord through node holes over rubber grommets on a CNC-routed black walnut frame, stainless support rods tucked neatly along the rows, two pairs of mallets beside the instrument, satin walnut and brushed aluminum textures, clean studio background, soft directional lighting, ~50mm lens, high detail.

## In-Context Prompt

Photorealistic workshop test scene of the glockenspiel on a bench, natural light over the walnut frame, a player striking a pilot bar with a hard rubber mallet while C5, A5, and C7 pilot bars and a small tuner sit nearby, paracord and grommet mounting visible, spare mallets and deburring tools blurred in the background, careful prototype-validation mood, realistic hands, shallow depth of field, concept visualization only.

## Detail Prompt

Photorealistic macro close-up of the signature free-free bar mounting: a brushed aluminum bar with clean deburred node holes, low-stretch cord passing through, rubber grommets isolating the bar from the black walnut frame, stainless restraint rod visible below but not touching the bar, soft raking light across aluminum grain and walnut pores, high detail, no measurement marks.

## Negative / Avoid

No text, logos, watermarks, toy rainbow bars, xylophone wooden bars, resonator tubes, dimension callouts, unreadable fake note labels, impossible cord routing, extra fingers, or metal-on-metal contact at the supports.
