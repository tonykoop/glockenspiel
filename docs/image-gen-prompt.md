# Image-Gen Prompts

Concept image only; not fabrication authority. Do not include cut-ready dimensions, hole locations, or toolpaths.

## Hero Prompt

A finished concert glockenspiel photographed as a real physical object on a clean neutral light-gray studio sweep, 3/4 product-photography angle, soft directional studio key light with a gentle fill, ~50mm lens, photorealistic, high detail. The instrument is a two-row piano-keyboard layout built on a warm CNC-routed black-walnut frame with a satin oil finish and visible straight grain: a front row of natural bars and a slightly raised back row of accidental bars, like the white and black keys of a piano. The sound bars are bright, mill-finished 6061-T6 aluminum flat bars, one inch wide and a quarter inch thick, with a subtle metallic sheen and clean deburred ends — graduating smoothly in length from the longest low bar at the left to the shortest high bar at the right, giving a clear visual tuning gradient. Each flat bar is plain and un-arched (no undercut, no resonator tubes beneath), suspended by paracord threaded through small holes near each end and resting on small rubber grommets seated in the walnut rails, so the metal never touches the wood; a thin stainless-steel support rod runs the length of each row as a travel restraint. A pair of mallets with hard-rubber and brass heads rests to one side for scale. Realistic tabletop scale cues, no text, no labels.

## In-Context Prompt

The same walnut-and-aluminum glockenspiel staged on a maker's workbench in a bright woodworking shop, lit by soft natural window light from the side, shallow depth of field with the longest low bars in crisp focus and the shop fading to a warm blur behind. A hand holds one mallet just above the natural row mid-strike, the bright aluminum bars catching the light against the dark walnut frame. Environmental storytelling: a chromatic tuner and a coil of paracord rest on the bench nearby, walnut offcuts and a few loose rubber grommets scattered to one side. Concept visualization only, photorealistic, warm and inviting, no text.

## Detail Prompt

Macro close-up of a single bright aluminum bar suspended at one of its end holes, raking low-angle light skimming across the mill-finished metal to reveal fine brushed texture and the clean chamfered edge. The frame shows: the small drilled hole through the flat aluminum bar, the paracord passing through it, the soft rubber grommet cradling the bar, and the dark walnut frame pocket below with the thin stainless support rod alongside — clearly showing how the bar floats free of the wood for sustain. High detail, shallow depth of field, no measurements, no callout lines.

## Negative / Avoid

No text, logos, watermarks, or fake maker marks; no dimension callouts or measurement lines; no arched/undercut bars or resonator tubes under the bars; no impossible mounting; anatomically plausible hands if a player is shown.
